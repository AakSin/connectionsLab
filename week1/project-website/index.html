Inspiration

This project was created in response to the COVID crisis in India in March 2020. Plasma is the liquid component of blood and it is essential for recovering COVID patients. Throughout all of India, people were struggling to find donors for plasma. This app was meant to connect people with similar blood types in your city so that people with COVID could recover faster. The standard mode of reaching out for Plasma was just posting on social media groups which I found ineffective. Keeping that in mind, I sought to design a more effective solution in Figma (a prototyping software). I looked into similar apps and websites for inspiration.
